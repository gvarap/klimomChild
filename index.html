🌱 Why it matters

The First 13 Years Shape Everything

A child's nutrition during these years determines lifelong health, brain development, and growth. Small gaps today become big challenges tomorrow.

🧠

Brain Development

90% of a child's brain development happens before age 5. Right nutrition — especially protein and Vitamin D — is the single biggest factor in cognitive growth.

🦴

Strong Bones & Height

Calcium and Vitamin D during childhood directly determine peak bone mass. Children who miss out are at higher risk of fractures and stunted height.

🛡️

Immunity & Resilience

A well-nourished child falls sick less often. Vitamin A, zinc and quality protein build the immune defenses that keep kids active and learning.

📏

Healthy Weight

Under- and over-nutrition are both concerns. Knowing your child's ideal intake helps you make confident, informed choices every single day.

❓ Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the calculator and Klimom A2 milk for children.

What age range does this calculator support?
+
The calculator supports children aged 1 to 13 years. This covers the critical growth and development phases where A2 milk plays an important nutritional role. For infants under 1 year, we recommend consulting a paediatrician directly.
How is the daily milk recommendation calculated?
+
The recommendation is based on age groups aligned with WHO and ICMR dietary guidelines: 350ml/day for ages 1–3, 450ml/day for ages 4–8, and 600ml/day for ages 9–13. These reflect optimal protein and calcium intake from A2 milk for each stage.
What is A2 milk and why is it better for children?
+
A2 milk contains only the A2 beta-casein protein, naturally found in Gir cow milk. Unlike regular milk (which often contains A1 protein), A2 milk is easier to digest for most children, reduces digestive discomfort, and delivers the same rich nutrients — protein, calcium, vitamin D and A — without the inflammatory response some kids experience.
Is the WhatsApp report really free?
+
Yes, completely free! After using the calculator, click the WhatsApp button to send your personalised results to our team. You'll receive a detailed nutrition report plus guidance from our team within a few hours.
How fresh is Klimom A2 milk?
+
Klimom follows a strict milking-to-delivery cycle: cows are milked early morning, milk is glass-sealed and dispatched for same-day delivery in Hyderabad. There are no extended chilling cycles or storage — you receive milk within hours of milking, preserving maximum nutrition.
Where does Klimom deliver?
+
Klimom currently delivers across Hyderabad. Coverage includes Banjara Hills, Jubilee Hills, Kondapur, Gachibowli, Madhapur, Hitech City, and surrounding areas. Visit klimom.online or WhatsApp us to confirm delivery to your specific pin code.
